## Description
Tripzync helps you plan trips with clear, day-by-day itineraries.

Create a trip by entering your destination, dates, and budget. Tripzync generates a structured plan with morning, afternoon, and evening activities, then saves it so you can view and update it anytime.

Key features:
- Generate trip itineraries by destination, days, and budget tier
- View itinerary items with map coordinates
- Edit and reorder activities in your plan
- Regenerate itinerary suggestions for existing trips
- Save, browse, and delete trips from your account
- Sync your profile and trip data with your backend

Tripzync is designed to keep planning simple, organized, and practical.
